## Questions

Open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/shaiknoorullah/opsbench/discussions). Tag the right category:

- **Q&A** — usage questions
- **Ideas** — proposals for new teams, skills, agents
- **Show and tell** — share your custom team
- **General** — anything else
